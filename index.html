Dr. Christopher Herzog, Ophthalmology

Welcome to Eye Care for You! We provide complete vision care for adults and children throughout Genesee County in a comfortable and friendly environment. We stay abreast of the lastest techniques and offer the highest standards of care.

At the office of Dr. Christopher Herzog, we provide eye exams, disease diagnosis and treatment, laser surgery and other services. We also make sure to inform all patients of their options so that they'll make the best decision for their eye health. We are fully committed to patient satisfaction and care.
